Common questions

Where do the credits come from? +
From Fannie Mae or Freddie Mac — whichever offers the better terms. They're price credits created when the agency waives loan-level price adjustments for eligible buyers.
Are they taxable or repaid? +
Neither. The credits are a pricing benefit on your loan, not income and not a loan — there's nothing to pay back and no second lien.
Is a homebuyer course required? +
No. A homeownership education course is not required for this program.
How much will I actually get? +
It depends on the tract, your loan amount, LTV, and credit profile. Send me your details and I'll give you a real figure for this property.
